Mechanical Engineering

What is this course about?

The Mechanical Engineering degree at Canterbury Christ Church University is designed to mold highly skilled, work-ready graduates who can contribute innovatively to various industries. This program employs the CDIO approach, developed by MIT, to provide hands-on, practical learning experiences. Students tackle real-world engineering challenges, developing creative solutions under the guidance of engineering experts. The curriculum focuses on applying science, technology, and math in real-world applications, preparing students to become effective, industry-ready engineers aimed at becoming Incorporated Engineers.
