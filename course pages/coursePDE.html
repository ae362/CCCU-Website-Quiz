Product Design Engineering

What is this course about?

The Product Design Engineering degree at Canterbury Christ Church University blends creative flair with technical prowess, preparing students to innovate practical solutions with commercial viability. This industry-focused degree emphasizes critical thinking and collaboration, using the CDIO approach developed by MIT to integrate engineering and design through real-world projects. Students learn to conceive, design, implement, and operate within various sectors including consumer products, aerospace, and health.
