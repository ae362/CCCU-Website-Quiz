Game Design

What is this course about?

The Games Design degree at Canterbury Christ Church University offers a dynamic approach to becoming a professional in the gaming industry. This program delves into the art and technology of video games, covering a broad spectrum from design principles to technical development for various multimedia platforms like computers, mobiles, and consoles. Students will learn to create innovative, unique, and complex video games while developing critical thinking and writing skills. The course is highly practical, allowing students to build a comprehensive portfolio that showcases their creativity and technical abilities. Additionally, the curriculum includes studies in games psychology and gaming culture, with opportunities for specialization as students advance, enhancing both their skills and industry knowledge.
